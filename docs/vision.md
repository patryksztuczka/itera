# Vision

## The Problem

In an era of information abundance and advanced AI, we are paradoxically losing the ability to truly _know_. We consume vast amounts of data daily, skimming through complex topics with the help of LLMs. While we often feel a sense of immediate understanding, this "knowledge" is fleeting. A few days later, the concepts evaporate, leaving us with a shallow grasp of what we thought we had mastered. We have become efficient at consuming information, but we have failed at retaining and integrating it.

## Our Goal

The goal of this system is to transform passive consumption into deep, lasting mastery. We aim to provide a tool that doesn't just present information, but ensures it stays with you, evolves within your mind, and becomes a part of your intellectual foundation.

We believe that deep learning is achieved through two primary pillars:

1.  **Retention through Spaced Repetition:** By leveraging the science of how our brains forget, we ensure that foundational facts and core concepts are reinforced at the optimal moment, preventing them from fading into the background.
2.  **Verification through Socratic Dialogue:** Real understanding is more than just memorization. We challenge the learner to explain, synthesize, and apply their knowledge. By acting as a rigorous interlocutor, the system ensures that a learner only progresses once they can provide a concise, deep, and true explanation of the subject matter.

This tool is for anyone who refuses to settle for shallow understanding—the lifelong learners who want to not only read, but to truly understand and remember.
